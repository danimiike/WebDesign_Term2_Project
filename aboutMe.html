My name is Danielle, I've known my husband Edson for 20 years. I am from Rio de Janeiro but I lived in Sao Paulo since I was 4 years old, He is from Sao Paulo. We started dating in 1999 on a trip to Ilha Comprida, south coast of Sao Paulo. We got married in 2005. After a few years enjoying life as a couple, we realized that something was missing to complete this union, and 6 years after the wedding, in December / 2011, our dear Gustavo was born. We saw our life change completely and that little human being made us see life with different perspectives, other purposes. But there was still a piece missing in our puzzle, and then two years later, in January / 2014 we were presented with Ian.
